“The first thing we do, let's kill all the lawyers.”

RBG was a huge Shakespeare fan and even made cameos in a few professional produtions. She performed in Henry IV and said of her role: "I had Dick the butcher’s part, with the famous line, ‘First thing we do, let’s kill all the lawyers.’ And then I asked if I could ad-lib an addition, I checked this out in advance, and the addition was, 'and next the reporters.'"
